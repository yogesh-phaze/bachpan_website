Achievement Track

Building Olympiad-ready thinkers, one week at a time.

Structured practice in Math, Science, English and Cyber Olympiads — woven into the regular timetable, not an extra burden after school.

➗

Math Olympiad

Logical reasoning, puzzles and speed-math, from IMO and SOF-pattern question sets.

🔬

Science Olympiad

Concept-based questions on EVS and Science topics with real-world application.

📖

English Olympiad

Grammar, vocabulary and comprehension practice through weekly reading challenges.

💻

Cyber Olympiad

Age-appropriate computer knowledge and safe-internet awareness for young learners.

How training works
1

Weekly practice sets

Short, grade-wise worksheets built into class time — not extra homework.

2

Monthly mock tests

Timed practice papers that mirror the real Olympiad format.

3

1-on-1 doubt mentoring

Teachers review each child's mistakes and coach on the concept, not just the answer.
