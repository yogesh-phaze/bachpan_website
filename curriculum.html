Academics

A curriculum that grows with your child.

From first alphabets in LKG to confident independent learners by Class 5 — every stage is designed around how young children actually learn.

LKG

Learning through play

Phonics, rhymes, number sense, motor skills and social habits — built through stories, songs and hands-on play, not worksheets.

UKG

Building school readiness

Early reading and writing, basic math operations, EVS discovery and structured group activities.

Class 1–2

Core foundations

English, Maths, EVS and Hindi with daily reading practice, and an introduction to computer basics.

Class 3–5

Independent thinkers

English, Maths, Science, Social Studies, Hindi and Computers, with project work, GK and Olympiad-aligned practice built into weekly classes.

Every subject, every stage

What's taught, in plain terms

English Mathematics Science / EVS Social Studies Hindi Computer Basics Art & Craft General Knowledge Physical Education Value Education

Assessments are continuous, not just exam-day — regular quizzes, projects and class participation all count.

How we teach

Three habits behind every classroom

🗣️

Speak first, write later

Especially in LKG–UKG, spoken confidence in English comes before formal writing.

🧩

Concept before formula

Maths and Science are taught with objects and examples before symbols and rules.

📅

Small, steady steps

Weekly learning goals instead of last-minute exam cramming, tracked with parents.
