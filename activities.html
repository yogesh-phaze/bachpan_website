Beyond the classroom

A hobby for every kind of child.

Not every child shines with a textbook first — our activity clubs give every student a place to discover what they're good at.

Classical and freestyle sessions, twice a week.

🎵

🎭

Drama Club

Storytelling and stage-acting practice for budding performers.

How clubs work

Twice-weekly sessions

Each club meets twice a week during the school activity period.

No extra fee

Clubs are included as part of regular school fees, not a paid add-on.

Open to switch

Children can try a new club each term to find what they enjoy most.

Curious which club fits?

Ask us about your child's interests.

Our activity coordinators are happy to guide you on the right club to start with.
