Home / Digital Library
Powered by AI

A library that reads your child as they read it.

Our digital library uses AI to match every story, worksheet and quiz to a child's own reading level — so no one is bored, and no one is lost.

🎯

Adaptive reading levels

The AI tracks what a child reads comfortably and gently raises the difficulty as they improve.

🦉

AI reading buddy

An in-app assistant explains tricky words, asks questions, and encourages kids as they read.

📚

1,000+ e-books

Illustrated stories, EVS readers and picture books, organised by grade and topic.

❓

Instant quizzes

Short, auto-generated comprehension quizzes after every story to check understanding.

📈

Progress for parents

A simple monthly summary shows what your child read and how their reading is improving.

🔊

Read-aloud mode

Text-to-speech narration for LKG–UKG children who are still learning to read independently.

A typical library session
Step 1

Child logs in with their class ID

No passwords to remember — a simple picture-based login for young students.

Step 2

AI suggests 3 books

Chosen from their reading level and interests — animals, space, festivals and more.

Step 3

Read, then a 3-question quiz

Quick and low-pressure, focused on comprehension rather than testing.

Step 4

Badges and streaks

Small rewards for consistent reading, visible to both the child and their teacher.
